About Five Corners, WA

Five Corners, WA is the unincorporated community located in the heart of Clark County. This charming and quaint area has been home to many residents since the early 1900s. The conservation area at Five Corners allows visitors to enjoy fishing, canoeing, and picnicking along the river that flows through this tranquil suburb. The schools in Five Corners also provide a range of educational opportunities for students of all ages, with kindergarten through high school options available. Additionally, various businesses offer employment that caters to members of the community as well as visitors. With access to grocery stores, restaurants, and other conveniences, many families find that living in Five Corners is a great option. In sum, this small part of Washington offers its citizens a place to call home that is filled with opportunity along with breathtaking views of nature.
