Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my oven needs to be repaired?

If your oven is not heating up correctly, not maintaining a consistent temperature, or exhibiting any other unusual behavior, then it may be in need of repair. It is also a good idea to check your oven’s owner’s manual for any troubleshooting tips or maintenance instructions. If the issue persists, it may be best to contact a professional appliance repair technician to inspect and diagnose the issue.

How long does an oven repair usually take?

An oven repair can take anywhere from a few hours to a few days depending on the type of repair that needs to be done. Some oven repairs can be done in one day, while others may require multiple visits from a technician and take longer to complete.

What should I do if my oven won't turn on?

If your oven won't turn on, you should first check the power cord and make sure it is plugged in securely. If it is, then you should check the circuit breaker and make sure that it is not tripped. If it is, then reset it and try to turn the oven on again. If the oven still won't turn on, then you should contact a qualified technician to diagnose and repair the issue.

What causes the oven to make loud noises when in use?

Loud noises from an oven can be caused by a few different things. One possible cause is the fan blades hitting something inside the oven. Another possible cause is a faulty heating element. Finally, the noise could be coming from a worn out bearing in the fan motor.
